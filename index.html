About

Who we are

In 1979 the school was bought by a company formed for the purpose, Gabi Limited. At that time the school consisted of the house, the additional classrooms, a carport, and a small playing field with eucalyptus trees at the bottom end. What is now the Foundation Stage building had walls but no roof. There were a total of 79 children on roll and six teachers. The name was changed to simply Gabi School.
