We are planning on supporting our own data as an institution through a proposed server room. The project was voted for unanimously and a go ahead directive given. The facility will support our online presence as well as research data we plan to support from tertiary institutions. This is in a bid to link up Gabi with higher learning institutions for mutual benefit. This will also promote digital literacy among our students and staff.
The launch of the committee room was an outstanding success. It was sponsored by various stakeholders including: Teachers, Parents, our Alumni Association, Ministry of Education and the general public. We wish to thank all involved in planning and renovation. We couldn't achieve this fete without you.
